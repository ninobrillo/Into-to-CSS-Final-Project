PLANNING TO ADOPT?

  • CHECKLIST FOR NEW ADOPTERS

    Here are some of the checklist you need to consider before adpoting a pet.
    1. Learn as much as you can about your new pet.
    2. Create a schedule and assign tasks.
    3. Choose your words wisely.
    4. Set expectations and boundaries.
    5. Pet-proof the house.
    6. Read up on training—especially, housebreaking.
    7. Choose a veterinarian.
    8. Buy supplies as a family.
    9. Meet other pet owners in the neighborhood.

  • PET ADAPTION FAQS

    Few questions you need to ask yourself and learn before adpopting.
    1. What's the pet's average life span?
    2. How much free time do you have available each day?
    3. How much space do you have?
    4. How much money can you spend?
    5. How often are you home?
    6. How frequently do you travel?
    7. How active are you?
    8. What type of vet care is available?
    9. Does your residence restrict certain pets?
    10. Are you allergic?
